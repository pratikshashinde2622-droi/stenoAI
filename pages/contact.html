FAQ

Is StenoAI free to use? ▼
Yes! StenoAI is completely free. No sign-up or payment required.
Is my data stored anywhere? ▼
No! Everything runs in your browser. No audio or text is ever uploaded to any server.
Which browsers are supported? ▼
Voice mode works best on Chrome or Edge. Text mode works on most modern browsers.
Can I add more languages? ▼
Contact us with your language request! We plan to support more languages based on user demand.
