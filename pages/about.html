About StenoAI

The story behind our little project

S

Smart Transcription for Everyone

StenoAI is a browser-based transcription platform built by two students who wanted a free, private, and powerful tool. Real-time voice recognition, audio recording, multi-language support — all without any server.

🎙

Real-Time Transcription

Watch your words appear instantly as you speak with high-accuracy speech recognition.

🔒

100% Private

Everything runs in your browser. No audio or text is ever sent to any external server.

🌐

13 Indian Languages

Full support for Hindi, Marathi, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Gujarati and more.

💾

Complete Export

Download transcript, summary and audio recording all in one click.

⌨️

Dual Input Modes

Live voice recording OR manual typing — whatever works best for you.

📊

Live Statistics

Track word count, line count, duration and speaker stats in real time.

Our Mission

We believe transcription should be accessible, accurate, and private. StenoAI was built to empower professionals — journalists, lawyers, educators, and students — with a free, powerful tool that respects their data and their time.
